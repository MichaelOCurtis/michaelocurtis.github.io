Solar Power

Photovoltaic solar panels convert sunlight into solar electric power. This solar electricity is used to run all electrical devices within a building. Solar design and install photovoltaic solar power systems to be grid connected. This allows for surplus electrical solar power to be sold to any electric power provider negating the need for expensive batteries. A range of high quality, high performance photovoltaic solar panels are available from Solar which can be fitted "on-roof" or "within-roof". As an accredited supplier and installer our installed solar power systems benefit from the maximum government solar power grant available. Solar Power supplies and installs residential and commercial Photovoltaic solar projects.

Solar Heating

Solar water heating is when daylight is converted into heat by solar panels normally located on the roof. This heat is stored within a solar hot water cylinder where it can be used to provide domestic hot water or underfloor heating within a building. Established in 1987, and its network of dealers have installed over 6000 solar hot water systems throughout the UK and Ireland. The company is fully accredited, demonstrating its commitment to provide high standards in products and installations. Solar handle residential and commercial projects involving residential hot water, underfloor heating, solar heating for swimming pools, process hot water and commercial solar water heating.
